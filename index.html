﻿What makes me unique?


​My passion for healthcare
​

​​My passion for healthcare sparked 8 years ago when I was in my Sophomore year of high school. I joined a school initiative where we sponsored 15 children in providing support and funding for their basic necessities - food, water, and healthcare. From then onwards, I was absolutely amazed at the results we were able to achieve. My school ultimately helped 15 children live and lead a better life for the future.

​I believe in bridging the gap between those who have the capability to give, and those who require the necessities the live. I believe in doing more firsthand to create a better future for everyone. I believe in the power of people, the power of change, and most importantly, the power of impact. 


​My ability to tell stories

Growing up, I have always been a creative individual at heart with a true passion for writing. My favourite thing to do as a child was to write...all the time. Being an avid piano performer and a visual artist, my writing is strongly influenced by my unique perspectives and differing mindset.

I love combining my marketing expertise with my ability to create exciting and influential content to portray a captivating story to the audience. 

My inspiration comes from a multitude of things, ranging from day-to-day events to hearing other individuals' personal experiences. Sometimes, my inspiration also comes from just my plain old imagination.
