Why am I so passionate about Watsi?

Because Watsi inspires me


I’m passionate about healthcare and I’m passionate about change. If you put the two together, that’s where Watsi comes in. Simply put, Watsi’s mission inspires me. All of you working at Watsi inspire me. But most importantly, the positive impact Watsi has left on thousands of people around the world inspires me, and I want to be a part of that change. I want to be able to help people firsthand and deliver to them what they need most. For someone that’s been passionate about making a difference to the world, accidentally landing on Watsi’s homepage was one of the best things that ever happened to me.
 
I believe that everyone deserves access to basic necessities, and healthcare is a primary one. Inequality will always be prevalent, and there will always be those who are better off and those who barely make it through the day. Nonetheless, seeing the mass amount of people who have donated to Watsi since its launch, I have strong faith in humanity that this inequality can continue to be leveraged – we can still make something good out of those who have resources to share, and leave something good for those in pressing need. I’m excited to continue making this change, and I’m truly excited to bring my passion onboard to show Watsi what I have to offer.
